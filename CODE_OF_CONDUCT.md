# Code of Conduct

We want a welcoming, professional, safety-first community.

## Expectations
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; ask questions before making accusations.
- Prioritize technician safety, privacy, and clarity.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or threats
- Sharing private jobsite footage without explicit permission
- Encouraging unsafe practices

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to keep the community safe and productive.
